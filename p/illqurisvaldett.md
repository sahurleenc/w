**Illqurisvaldett and Austrakildes** (**IQA**), officially the **Sovereign-Federation of Illqurisvaldett and Austrakildes**, is a country located in Southern Onaten and Virgauria. Illqurisvaldett and Austrakildes is the second largest country in the world after Ambrosia, with its territory both completely encircling the Hae'an-Sonn Sea and partially encircling the Sokkdypt Sea. Illqurisvaldett and Austrakildes borders Seohwa and Kamikaji to the northwest, Jamesville, Virtaria, Issasuku, and Ambrosia to the north, Reinhoute to the east, and the Southern Sea in the south. Additionally, it has maritime boundaries with Ainonjin to the west through the Sanwa Sea. It has a population of 218 million, also making it the fourth most populous nation after Ainonjin, Ambrosia, and Dosnia.

The federal government of Illqurisvaldett and Austrakildes is a presidential constitutional federal republic and representative democracy. It has a bicameral national legislature known as the Grand Forum composed of the lower house of the Auditorium and the upper house of the Balcony. The Union Law also grants a substansial degree of autonomy to the country's 38 prefectures, as well as significant autonomy to the remaining 8 territories and 2 independent cities of Caisei and the capital of Akopenhagen. Since the 1990s, the framework of federal politics have been dominated by the Illqurisvaldett Conservatives, Labour, and The Greens.

A developed country and an established superpower, Illqurisvaldett and Austrakildes ranks high in economic competitiveness, innovation, and higher education. The economy of Illqurisvaldett and Austrakildes is the largest in the world by nominal GDP since 1992, accounting for a fourth of nominal global GDP. The country is also a global leader in automotives, electronics, industry, and robotics, and has made significant contributions to science and technology. Illqurisvaldett and Austrakildes have also made massive involvement in healthcare, having one of the world's highest life expectancies, though its wealth inequality is highly pronounced. The country has one of the most strongest and developed militaries in the world, having one of the largest defense budgets. The culture of Illqurisvaldett and Austrakildes is well known around the world, particularly its pop culture in architecture, traditions, cuisine, entertainment, fashion, animation, films, and television.
